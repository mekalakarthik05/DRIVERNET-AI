DATA PROCESSING

Augmentation Pipeline

Robust data augmentation ensures the model generalizes across diverse road conditions.

✂️

Crop

Remove sky and car hood from the frame. Focus the CNN on the road surface and lane markings only.

image[60:-25, :, :]
📐

Resize

Scale down to 66×200 pixels using area interpolation for optimal feature extraction.

cv2.resize(img, (200, 66))
🎨

RGB → YUV

Convert to YUV color space. Separates luminance from chrominance, matching NVIDIA's approach.

cv2.cvtColor(img, RGB2YUV)
🔄

Random Flip

Horizontally flip with 50% probability and negate the steering angle to balance left/right turns.

cv2.flip(img, 1)
↔️

Translation

Random shift simulates off-center driving and teaches recovery behavior.

cv2.warpAffine(img, M, …)
☀️

Brightness

Randomly adjust brightness in HSV space to handle varying lighting conditions.

hsv[:,:,2] *= ratio
